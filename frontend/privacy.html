Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Information We Collect

When you leave a comment on our blog, we collect your name and the content of your comment. We do not collect your email address.

How We Use Your Information

Your name and comment are displayed publicly on the blog post page after an administrator approves them. We use this information solely for the purpose of enabling reader discussions.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies essential for the admin authentication system (Laravel Sanctum). No tracking or analytics cookies are used at this time.

Data Storage

All data is stored in a SQLite database on our server. We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.

Your Rights

You may request the removal of your comment at any time by contacting the site administrator. We will comply within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out via the admin dashboard or contact the site owner directly.
